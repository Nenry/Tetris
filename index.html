How to play:
  • Use the tile pieces to fill up a row. That row will clear and points will be rewarded. Multiple rows filled up at one time will receieve bonus scores
Instructions
  • Press P to Start/Pause/Unpause the game.
  • Press Q/W to rotate, back or forward.
  • Press space to do fast drop
  • Press R to Restart the game
